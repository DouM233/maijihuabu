# Image Analysis Checklist

Use this checklist for every uploaded image set.

## Step 1: Visible facts

- What product is shown?
- What is clearly visible?
- What is partly visible?
- What is not visible?

## Step 2: Product attributes

- Product
- Material
- Color
- Shape
- Surface finish
- Logo or text visibility

## Step 3: Scene attributes

- Camera angle
- Framing
- Lighting
- Background
- Composition
- Presence of model or hands

## Step 4: Commercial attributes

- Hero image
- Usage image
- Feature image
- Detail image
- Gift image
- Comparison image

## Step 5: Fact versus inference

For each important style statement, decide whether it is:
- visible fact
- strong inference
- weak inference

## Step 6: Prompt readiness

Before writing the prompt, confirm:
- product identity is stable
- material is stable
- color is stable
- composition is stable
- brand style is stable
- no invented content is being carried into the prompt
