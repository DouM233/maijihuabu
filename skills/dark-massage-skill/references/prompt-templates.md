## Template 1: Sofa Sunset Lumbar Editorial

```text
GLOBAL TONE LOCK: This image must feel like a premium apartment at sunset around [time], dimly lit, intimate, and filled with deep amber-honey warmth. Rich warm shadows are required. If it looks bright, cheerful, or like daytime, it is wrong.

HIGH-END CAMPAIGN KV — [product_name] on a caramel velvet sofa in a luxury apartment. Editorial lifestyle campaign quality for Taobao or JD.

MODEL:
European Caucasian woman, [age], fair luminous skin, [hair_style], minimal natural makeup, [eye_state], relaxed premium editorial expression.
Outfit: [outfit_description]. Fabric must read as satin-silk with visible sheen.
Pose: [pose_anchor].

PRODUCT:
[product_name], mint-sage green body, dark green piping, bow-tie hourglass silhouette, plush thickness around 8-10 cm, [control_panel_visibility].
Placement: [exact placement].
Visibility: [visibility target].
Hard rule: [contact or placement rule].

ENVIRONMENT:
Warm caramel-toffee velvet sofa, floor-to-ceiling windows with horizontal blinds, dark wood furniture, warm-toned floor lamp, dark hardwood floor, clean premium apartment, no clutter.

LIGHTING:
Low-angle sunset light through blinds from [light_direction]. Warm amber highlights on skin and satin. Deep warm brown shadows in background and away from window. Blind shadows stay soft and tasteful.

COMPOSITION:
Wide environmental lifestyle shot, vertical 3:4 or 4:5, eye-level, subject occupies around 50-60 percent of frame, background softly blurred, medium-format 85 mm feel, f/2.8 to f/4.

QUALITY LOCK:
8K RAW photorealistic, physically accurate lighting, satin sheen, velvet texture, skin subsurface scattering, no AI smoothing.

NEGATIVE:
bright daylight, overexposed room, cool blue tones, green sofa, plastic texture, square cushion, cluttered kitchen, cartoon render, weak product visibility, [scene-specific negatives].
```

Best for:
- sofa lean-back lumbar scenes
- stretch and relax lifestyle hero shots
- product-plus-model campaign KV

## Template 2: Home Office Dusk Lumbar Lifestyle

```text
GLOBAL TONE LOCK: Early evening home-office scene at dusk with rich orange-amber window light, noticeable contrast between lit and shaded sides, and a calm slow-down mood. Not midday, not fluorescent office light.

HIGH-END LIFESTYLE CAMPAIGN — [product_name] used as lumbar support in a warm residential workspace.

MODEL:
European Caucasian woman, [age], fair skin, [shoulder-length hair variant], gentle relaxed expression, eyes [open or softly closed].
Outfit: caramel-brown blazer, cream inner layer, light khaki trousers, or [approved office outfit].
Pose: seated on [chair_type], torso turned about 30 degrees, leaning naturally back into the cushion, [arm anchor], legs parallel and relaxed.

PRODUCT:
Mint-green [product_name], hourglass silhouette, dark green piping, readable 6-button control panel on exposed side.
Placement: upright against chair backrest at lower back.
Visibility: around 55-65 percent visible with the exposed side as hero.
Hard rule: zero gap between back and cushion.

ENVIRONMENT:
Deep wood desk, laptop, ceramic cup, horizontal blinds, warm wall tones, framed abstract art, residential home-office styling.

LIGHTING:
Primary key light from window blinds on [window side], rich dusk amber around 2800-3000K, visible shape on face, blazer, and cushion, deeper warm shadow on the far side.

COMPOSITION:
Medium-wide 3/4 lifestyle shot, eye-level, vertical 3:4 or 4:5, desk and window both readable, background softly blurred.

QUALITY LOCK:
8K RAW, physically correct dusk lighting, visible woven fabric texture, readable wood grain, realistic skin and product detail.

NEGATIVE:
corporate swivel chair, mesh ergonomic chair, typing pose, hunched posture, cold office lighting, bright white wall, crossed legs, hidden cushion, floating cushion, [scene-specific negatives].
```

Best for:
- work-from-home relief
- long-sitting pain point images
- office relaxation story-driven ecommerce images

## Template 8: Lumbar Fit Comparison Split-Screen

```text
GLOBAL TONE LOCK: Premium comparison graphic with the same dark warm visual system as the main campaign images. Both panels must feel cohesive, not like mixed sources.

COMMERCIAL COMPARISON IMAGE — split-screen showing how [product_name] supports the lumbar area in a warm premium environment.

LAYOUT:
Two-panel composition with rounded-rectangle cards or clean vertical split.
Left panel: real lifestyle side view showing product against lower back.
Right panel: simplified support explanation, x-ray-style overlay, or alternate support angle.
Background outside the panels can be deep warm brown or near-black warm tone.

LEFT PANEL:
European Caucasian woman seated in [sofa or chair], torso upright but relaxed, product supporting lumbar curve.
Mint-green product clearly visible, control panel readable if angle allows.
Warm amber lighting, realistic body contact, visible curve support story.

RIGHT PANEL:
Choose one:
- anatomical overlay with red support zone
- alternate side angle showing fit
- schematic-style comfort comparison with the product hugging the lumbar area

PRODUCT RULE:
The product shape and color must match the left panel exactly.
The lumbar support story must be visually obvious in one second.

LIGHTING:
Keep warm amber consistency on the photographic panel. Keep the explanatory panel visually compatible, not cold gray.

COMPOSITION:
Vertical 1:1, 4:5, or panel-friendly ecommerce crop.
Use clean margins, rounded corners, and strong separation between panels.

QUALITY LOCK:
Sharp readable product edges, realistic body alignment, premium graphic composition, no cheap infographic look.

NEGATIVE:
mismatched product shape, blue-gray medical diagram, flat lighting, unreadable product, busy layout, random arrows everywhere, [scene-specific negatives].
```

Best for:
- 腰椎贴合说明
- 功能对比图
- detail page explanatory panels

## Reusable Blocks

### Product block

```text
[product_name], mint-sage green fabric body, dark green piping, bow-tie hourglass silhouette, plush thickness around 8-10 cm, realistic compression, readable control panel, breathable mesh texture where appropriate.
```

### Quality block

```text
8K RAW photorealistic, physically accurate lighting, natural skin subsurface scattering, visible satin sheen, visible velvet or mesh texture, no AI smoothing, no CGI or cartoon look, print-campaign ready.
```

### Base negatives

```text
bright daylight, overexposed lighting, cool blue tones, fluorescent office look, green sofa, wrong product color, square cushion, plastic texture, hidden product, cluttered background, kitchen appliances, cartoon render, anime style, game-CG look.
```

## Suggested Fill Order

When you add a new prompt, fill fields in this order:
1. tone family
2. scenario sentence
3. product placement and visibility
4. pose anchor
5. environment anchors
6. scene-specific negatives
